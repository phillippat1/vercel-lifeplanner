Key Terms Explained
What Is Coast FIRE?

Coast FIRE is the point where your invested assets are large enough that — even with ZERO additional contributions — compound growth alone will grow your portfolio to your full retirement number by your target retirement age.

Once you hit your Coast FIRE number, you only need income to cover current living expenses. You can switch to passion work, part-time, or lower-stress roles without worrying about retirement.

✅ Freedom to choose lower-paying but fulfilling work
✅ Reduces financial stress — your future is already secured
✅ Enables career pivots, part-time work, or entrepreneurship
✅ Protects against burnout by removing the "must save" pressure
